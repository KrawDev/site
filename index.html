About $KRAW

Born in the depths of wen.markets, $KRAW clawed it's way into reality. Now it lives on Polygon to remind us: nothing is safe, not even this site.

$KRAW is not a meme. It’s an anti-meme. A feathered slap in the face to utility-maxi narratives and roadmap worshippers. We exist to troll the very idea of structure, safety, and sanity in crypto.

There’s no whitepaper. No VC backing. No promise of generational wealth. Only chaos, confusion, and the occasional squawk.

Rug jokes? That’s the point. We don’t rug, we just keep pretending we will. That’s the real rug.

Our Discord is home to degens, trolls, and the clinically unfazed. Play our games like Krawpoopers, Krappy Bird, and Krawdle. Win, farm, or just lose your mind with us.

$KRAW isn’t about making it. It’s about mocking the idea of "making it" and laughing while everything burns.
